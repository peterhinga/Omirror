Contact and reporting child abuse

This site is fully compliant with the Law of Finland. The administrator is removing any links to child abuse content. Please 1. email us the exact URL of the content you suspect to be child abuse, and 2. report anonymously to the Internet Watch Foundation..

Updated policy in October 2023: There is now a filtering of all sexually related searchers due to the unfortunate massive distribution and search of child sexual abuse on Tor. If the search contains these terms, then Ahmia.fi refuses to show the results. Instead, the search offers a link to a self-help programme. The self-help programme is primarily intended for people who are worried about their interests, thoughts, feelings, or actions concerning children. For example, „jailbait“ is one of these filtered terms. As a result, Ahmia.fi is offering a link to a self-help programme: https://ahmia.fi/search/?q=jailbait In addition, Ahmia applies an automated text mining algorithm to remove possible links to child abuse from the search results.
